Why this works

  • Events have urgency, social proof, and better margins than rail.
  • Transfer mechanics are easier to explain and verify.
  • Metro cities and campuses concentrate both supply and demand.

Core loop

  1. Seller uploads proof.
  2. Eligibility engine checks transferability.
  3. Buyer pays into escrow.
  4. Seller transfers officially.
  5. Buyer confirms and payout is released.
